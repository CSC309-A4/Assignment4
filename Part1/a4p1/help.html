Interactions


  • press logo on top left to return to homepage
  • sign in will drop down links to user sign in and deliverer sign in
  • login will take you to log in page
  • help will take you to this page
  • In Homepage, Order Now will take you to order page
  • In Order page, Submitting an order will take you to a sample confirmation page
  • In Order Confirmation Page, Confirm Order will take you to Order Form
  • In User Sign Up and Login, Getting Started or Log In will take you to a sample user profile
  • In Delivery Sign Up, Sign In will take you to a sample deliverer profile


Specifications

Homepage: index.html
User Sign In/Log In/Profile: user.html, login.html, userProfile.html
Deliverer Sign In/Log In/Profile: deliverySignUp.html, login.html, deliveryProfile.html
Order Page: order.html, orderConfirmation.html, orderForm.html
