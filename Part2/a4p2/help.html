Hi there

Do you need help with getting started? Read on then.

  • Sign up: click on the sign up page at the top of your screen. You can sign up as either a user or a deliverer.

  • Login: After signing up, login by entering your username or password. Remember to indicate whether you are a user or deliverer.

  • Users: You can place an order by going to your profile page. Fill out the order form to place an order.

  • Deliverers: You can accept an order by going to your profile page and searching for any outstanding orders. You will receive information about this order and contact information for the user who made the order.

  • Additionally, you can leave feedback about any user or deliverer by going to the feedback page, a link to the feedback page can be found on your profile page.

  • When you are finished, you can log out by pressing the logout button at the top of your page.
